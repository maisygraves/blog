LEARNING IN PUBLIC

Hi, I'm Maisy Graves.

I translate complex information into accurate and meaningful stories, provide insights that support informed, impactful decisions and bring complexity and nuance to light. I'm especially interested in bridging disciplines — using data, ethics, and clear argumentation to bring complex, fragmented problems into sharper focus. I believe that better thinking comes from better questions, not tighter silos.

This blog serves several purposes - a tool for learning and reflection as I hone my skills, a record of the work and people shaping my knowledge and an invitation for feedback and redirection from those more knowledgable than myself. You can reach me on any of these platforms:
